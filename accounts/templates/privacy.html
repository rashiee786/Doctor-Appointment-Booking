MedBook Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 2026

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal details (name, email, phone number).
  • Medical information required for booking appointments.
  • Usage data to improve our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to schedule appointments, send reminders, and improve user experience. Your information is never sold to third parties.

3. Data Security

MedBook uses secure servers and encryption to protect your health records. Only authorized doctors and staff can access your data.

4. Sharing of Information

Your data may be shared with healthcare providers you book appointments with. We do not share your information with advertisers or unrelated third parties.

5. Your Rights

  • Request access to your personal data.
  • Correct inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.

6. Cookies & Tracking

MedBook may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work properly.

7. Updates

We may revise this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be posted on this page.
